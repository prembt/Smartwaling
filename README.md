# Municipality Hub
A digital directory to access all essential daily services within our municipality.

## Categories Included:
* Hospitals & Health Centers
* Educational Institutes
* Manpower & Consultants
* Government Offices
* Hotels & Lodging
* Shops (Grocery, Bakery, Clothing, etc.)
* Banks & ATMs
* Places to Visit
* Local Services (Plumbing, Electricians, etc.)

## How to use
Simply use the search bar to filter through the categories or click a tile to see more details.
